FAKHAR WOMAN - LATTAFA

種類...オードパルファム

総容量...100ml

容器の形状...スプレー

This is a new fragance. Fakhar Rose was launched in 2022. Top notes are: Fruits, Lily, Pomegranate and Aldehydes. The heart notes are: Tuberose, Jasmine, Gardenia, Honeysuckle, Ylang Ylang, Rose and Peony. The base notes are: Vanilla, White Musk, Ambroxan and Sandalwood. (Perfume inspired by L"Interdit Eau de Parfum by Givenchy)
